What makes a good offer?
  1. A real problem. New business your buyer is missing out on.
  2. What you'd do about it. And why your way beats the usual way.
  3. Pricing that favours the buyer. Cheaper than the alternative, or tied to results.
  4. One simple promise. A free thing first, pay after the work, pay per result, or a refund if we miss. Always just one, never stacked.
